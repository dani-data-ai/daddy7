Week 1: Changing a Child Changes You

Building the Foundation of Love and Connection

For Elias (7, shy, emotional, loves hugs):

  • Daily hugs ritual: 3 specific hug times - morning, after school, bedtime
  • Brave moments: Each day, acknowledge one "brave" thing he did (even small ones)
  • Nighttime routine: Special bedtime prayers together, night light with timer
  • Breathing games: Blow bubbles (great for asthma!) - see who can make the biggest bubble
  • Wrestling time: 10 minutes of gentle "knight training" with foam swords after school

For Lucas (9, independent, athletic, observant):

  • Man-to-man time: 15 minutes daily of just talking - let him lead the conversation
  • Football training: Practice skills that build lung capacity (dribbling, short sprints)
  • Observation game: Ask him what he noticed about people's behavior that day
  • Respect handshake: Teach firm handshake, practice daily
  • Problem-solving: Give him small family decisions to help with

For Miriam (3, cuddly, learning to talk):

  • Cuddle reading: 20 minutes of picture books together daily
  • Simple words: Practice saying family names clearly
  • Helper tasks: Let her "help" with simple chores (sorting socks by color)
  • Dance parties: 10 minutes of dancing to build coordination

üè† Family Activities:

  • Family motto: Create a simple family motto together
  • Dinner conversations: Each person shares one good thing from their day
  • Weekend adventure: One small outdoor activity together
